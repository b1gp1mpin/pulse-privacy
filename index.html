Privacy Policy

Pulse Fitness — Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Pulse Fitness ("Pulse", "we", "the app") is a personal fitness tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Your profile information (name, email, goals, experience level)
  • Workout sessions and rest day logs
  • Strength and cardio progress entries
  • Body weight measurements
  • Progress photos
  • Nutrition logs (meals, calorie targets, hydration)
  • Grocery lists
  • Personal goals

None of this data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. Your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

Pulse does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data services.

Camera & Photo Library

The app may request access to your device's camera and photo library for the purpose of taking and selecting progress photos. These photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Third-Party Services

Pulse does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app operates entirely offline.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. Since all data is stored locally, removing the app removes all associated data permanently.

Children's Privacy

Pulse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at pulsefitnesselite.app@gmail.com.
